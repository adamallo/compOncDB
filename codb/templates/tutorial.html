Neoplasia Prevalence Results Explained

Column NameExplanation
LocationUser specified tissue. "all" includes all tissues for that species. Only included if a specific location or tissue is examined.
RecordsWithDenominatorsTotal number of records for that species (and optionally species records for the given tissue) excluding records from databases which do not include non-neoplasia records. This will vary if the necropsy or infant records options are checked.
NeoplasiaWithDenominatorsTotal number of neoplasia records for that species (and optionally species records for the given tissue) excluding records from databases which do not include non-neoplasia records. This will vary if the necropsy or infant records options are checked.
NeoplasiaPrevalenceNeoplasiaWithDenominators divded by RecordsWithDenominators (tissue level results are divided by RecordsWithDenominators for all tissues).
MalignancyKnownTotal number of records for a malignant or benign determination could be made.
MalignantTotal number of malignant records from databases which include non-neoplasia records.
MalignancyPrevalenceMalignant divided by RecordsWithDenominators (tissue level results are divided by the species level total).
PropMalignantAll malignant records (regardless of source database) divided by MalignancyKnown.
BenignTotal number of benign records from databases which include non-neoplasia records.
BenignPrevalenceBenign divided by RecordsWithDenominators (tissue level results are divided by the species level total).
PropBenignAll benign records (regardless of source database) divided by MalignancyKnown.
AverageAge(months)Average age for all records from databases which include non-neoplasia records.
AvgAgeNeoplasia(months)Average age for neoplasia records from databases which include non-neoplasia records.
MaleNumber of male records from databases which include non-neoplasia records.
FemaleNumber of female records from databases which include non-neoplasia records.
MaleNeoplasiaNumber of male neoplasia records from databases which include non-neoplasia records.
FemaleNeoplasiaNumber of female neoplasia records from databases which include non-neoplasia records.
RecordsFromAllSourcesTotal number of records for that species (and optionally species records for the given tissue). This will vary if the necropsy or infant records options are checked.
NeoplasiaFromAllSourcesTotal number of neoplasia records for that species (and optionally species records for the given tissue). This will vary if the necropsy or infant records options are checked.
NecropsiesNumber of necrpsied records from databases which include non-neoplasia records.
#SourcesNumber of unique sources from all databases for that species.
NoTissueInfoNumber of neoplasia records without tumor location information.
